⌨️ Typing Test

Designed to help you master letters and words, elevating your typing speed to a professional level.

  • Real-time WPM Tracking: The timer starts automatically as soon as you begin typing. Watch your Words Per Minute (WPM) update live on the screen.
  • Focus on Accuracy: Speed is important, but accuracy is key. Every mistake is highlighted in red, allowing our system to calculate your exact accuracy percentage.
  • For All Skill Levels: Whether you are a beginner looking for basic words, an intermediate typist practicing complex vocabulary, or a programmer needing specific keywords, we have you covered.
  • Build Muscle Memory: Regular practice with this test helps you develop 'Touch Typing' techniques, allowing you to type effortlessly without looking down.

🔢 Number Practice

A specialized mode designed to help you operate the Number Pad (Numpad) quickly and flawlessly.

  • Data Entry & Accounting Focus: Numpad speed is crucial for users preparing for banking, accounting, and data entry jobs. We designed this mode specifically to enhance those essential skills.
  • Diverse Data Formats: Don't just practice plain numbers. Train with real-world data formats like phone numbers, dates, currency values, and percentages.
  • Step-by-Step Training: Progress through Easy, Medium, and Hard levels. Start with simple single digits and steadily build your speed up to complex inputs.
  • Time Management: Learn how to enter large amounts of numerical data in a short time, giving you a significant edge in competitive exams and real tasks.
